Long Beach is chock full of great places to eat and drink. Here are some of our favorite spots for coffee and food in downtown Long Beach:

Dining Recommendations:

Recreational Coffee (237 Long Beach Blvd Suite A) - Located right below our venue! Perfect for coffee, light meals, and casual dining.

Midnight Oil (532 E 1st St) - A local favorite for specialty coffee and all-day dining options. They also have 3 speakeasies for evening drinks!

Breakfast Bar (70 Atlantic Ave) - Great for breakfast, lunch, and hearty comfort food dishes.

The Ordinarie (210 The Promenade N) - Currently an amazing Halloween pop-up with great drinks and New American food. Trendy spot with creative offerings.

555 Steakhouse (555 E Ocean Blvd) - Upscale dining experience perfect for dinner and special occasions.

Buen Provecho (546 E 4th St) - Authentic Mexican cuisine serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner with traditional dishes.

Ammatoli (201 E 3rd St) - Mediterranean-inspired restaurant with fresh, flavorful options for lunch and dinner.

Beechwood Brewing (210 E 3rd St) - Local craft brewery with great beer selection and casual dining atmosphere.

ISM (4 Pine Ave) - Trendy cocktail bar and restaurant with innovative drinks and modern cuisine.

All of these spots are within walking distance of the venue and downtown hotels!

Long Beach is very walkable, especially around downtown and the waterfront. Parking, however, is notoriously terrible in most parts of the city. We highly recommend using rideshare apps like Uber or Lyft for convenience. The city of Long Beach also offers a free rideshares throughout the extended waterfront neighborhoods called Circuit, which can be downloaded from here: https://www.ridecircuit.com/long-beach

Long Beach Airport (LGB) is our top recommendation - it's very close to the venue (only six miles on side streets), and never crowded. Alternatively, John Wayne Airport (SNA) in Santa Ana is another smaller, less crowded airport, though it is a further drive on the freeway. Finally, Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) is about 30 minutes away and has more flight options, however it is very crowded.
